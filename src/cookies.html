Cookie Policy

Welcome to Echo-Edgez! To enhance your experience with our website, ensure its correct operation, and analyze user activity, we use cookies and similar technologies. This policy explains in detail what these technologies are, why we use them, and how you can manage them.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of data (text file) that a website saves on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit it. This allows the site to "remember" your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don't have to re-enter them every time you return.

What types of cookies do we use?

We classify the cookies used on the Echo-Edgez website into the following categories:

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for the website to function and provide access to secure sections.
  • Analytical: Collection of anonymous information about how visitors use our resource to optimize the site structure.
  • Functional: Allow us to remember your choices (language, region) to personalize your experience.
  • Marketing: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness.

Why do we use cookies?

The primary purposes of using cookies on the Echo-Edgez platform are:

  • Ensuring stable and secure operation of the digital infrastructure.
  • Analyzing user behavior to improve our AI tools.
  • Personalizing content within passive income programs.
  • Providing expert support based on your interests.

Your Choice and Management

You have full control over cookies. Most browsers allow you to delete and block them in the "Settings" or "Options" menu. However, disabling strictly necessary cookies may lead to the incorrect functioning of some features on our site.
