Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Storage

We collect information solely with your consent to provide high-quality service: booking consultations, participating in programs, and subscribing to insights. A complex set of modern organizational and technical security measures is used to protect your data.

Data is stored for the period necessary to provide the services or until the company ceases its activities. We perform timely destruction or anonymization of data to prevent its unauthorized use.
