Personal Data Policy

1. General Provisions

This Personal Data Processing Policy (hereinafter referred to as the "Policy") defines the procedure and conditions for processing personal data undertaken by the Echo-Edgez platform team (hereinafter referred to as the "Operator") and establishes measures to ensure the security of this data.

1.1. The Operator considers the observance of human and civil rights and freedoms when processing personal data, including the protection of rights to privacy and personal secrets, to be the most important goal and condition for its activities.

1.2. This Policy applies to all information that the Operator may receive about visitors to the echo-edgez.org website.

2. Key Definitions

  • Website — the collection of graphic materials and program codes of Echo-Edgez.
  • User — any visitor to our career blog.
  • Personal Data — any information relating to a specific User.
  • Consent — a voluntary expression of will for data processing (e.g., via a feedback form).

3. Data We Process

To provide expert support and AI analytics, we process:

  • Personal Information: Last name, first name, email address, contact phone numbers.
  • Automated Data: IP address, cookies, browser type, and visit analytics via Google Analytics.

4. Purposes of Data Processing

  • Identifying the User for access to career materials.
  • Establishing feedback for conducting consultations.
  • Informing about new growth strategies and AI solutions via Email.
  • Analyzing platform usage to improve digital infrastructure.

5. Legal Grounds

The Operator processes data only with the voluntary consent of the User, expressed through filling out forms on the echo-edgez.org website. Anonymized data is processed if permitted in your browser settings.

6. Security and Storage

Мы принимаем все необходимые технические меры для защиты данных от неправомерного доступа. We take all necessary technical measures to protect data from unauthorized access. Your data will never be shared with third parties, except in cases of compliance with UK and EU legislation.

The period for data processing is unlimited. You may withdraw your consent at any time.
